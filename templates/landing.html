How a name moves through this tool

  1. 01You submit a name.A single name here, or a full CSV once you're screening from your dashboard.
  2. 02It's normalized.Accents, punctuation, and word order are stripped out so spelling variants don't slip through.
  3. 03It's compared to every name and alias on file.Across the OFAC SDN List and the UN Consolidated List, scored 0–100 for similarity.
  4. 04Matches above your threshold come back flagged.For a person to review — never as an automatic accept or reject.
